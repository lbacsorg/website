BUILDING A CYBER AWARE WORKFORCE

Cybersecurity is everyone’s responsibility. In recognition of that, the Long Beach Alliance in Cybersecurity is providing research, education, and outreach to help train a cyber-aware and cyber-alert workforce to effectively protect local and national cyber interests such as Long Beach Airport, the port of Long Beach in addition to various local industries. Building upon the nationally recognized Long Beach College Promise—a four-way partnership between California State University Long Beach, Long Beach City College, Long Beach Unified School District, and the City of Long Beach – the Alliance’s comprehensive curriculum will serve the needs of the region’s diverse employers and create a next-generation workforce.
